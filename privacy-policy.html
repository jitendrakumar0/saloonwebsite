Cookies and web beacons
Our website uses "Cookies" so that we can customize our content and serve you with relevant information when you revisit our website. Cookies can be described as small bits of data that web servers send to your web browser, which it saves in text form. You have the option to choose whether to accept the cookies or not by toggling on/off your browser notification. We use the data thus collected to analyze and understand the user behavior to further improve our online services. It is to be noted that if you reject cookies, then it might restrict you from accessing certain sections or features of our website.
