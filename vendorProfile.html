About The Kalon Unisex salon

One of the top-rated Salons in Sector 29,Noida, Master Cut The Unisex Salon is a Über destination for Hair, Beauty and Grooming services with a firm foothold and reputation in the industry. Renowned for their edgy and stylish haircuts, trims, hair colours, hair treatments and ultra-lavish makeup and beauty services for Men and Women, it is just the ideal destination to try out the latest styles and trends. Master Cut The Unisex Salon has a pool of highly trained, skilled, and experienced artists and beauticians, who are masters in their respective roles.

Their wide assortment of hair, skin, beauty and personal care services are characterized by their attention to detail, warmth and concern. This is the reason why the establishment is able to garner a vast client base and continuously growing. If you are looking for trendy hairdo, hair spa, colour your hair, super cool highlights,a gorgeous blow dry or a complete new makeover, then look no further, as your search ends here at Master Cut The Unisex Salon.
